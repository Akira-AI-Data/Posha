## Data Safety

Data collected:
- Name
- Email address
- User IDs
- Photos uploaded for meal analysis
- Health and fitness information entered by the user
- App activity

Purpose:
- Account management
- App functionality
- Personalization
- Analytics and product improvement

Data shared:
- Photos and meal text may be sent to AI providers for meal analysis.
- Payment details are handled by Stripe; Posha does not store card numbers.

Security:
- Data is transmitted over HTTPS.
- Users can request account/data deletion through support.
